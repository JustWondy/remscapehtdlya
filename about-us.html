REMscape: ABOUT US


How to Dream Like You're On Acid, or better known as REMscape, is a dream archive meant to contextualize the unknown of your own mind. We believve that your mind is a powerful tool that often don't use while we fall asleep. The main goal of this website is to teach you how to be in-tune with your dreams, specifically lucid dreaming. Lucid dreaming feels like a public anomaly to us, so to the well-versed 'dreamers', you can become a seer to your own mind.
